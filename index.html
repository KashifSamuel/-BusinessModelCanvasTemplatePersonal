The Business Model Canvas

By: Kashif Samuel

Key Partners

-          Google Play and IPhone store

-          Xamarin

-          Retail Stores

-          Related Supporting Emergency procedures

-          Loyalty

 

Key Activities

-          Schedule a time frame for random emergency drill

-          Compete with other user for best response time

-          Engage with other mobile apps to sponsor The Drill

-          Give free app

-          Build loyalty

-          Build the app at lowest cost and aim for presale

Value Propositions

-          Provide free basic version of The Drill app and charge on add-on packs

-          Charge corporate level for licensing

-          Preparing for future emergency situations in challenging way

-          Build awareness for real life emergency situations

-          Emergency Drill application and add-on packs for customers emergency interest

-          Frequent updates for app upgrade and support through email

-          Customers will feel safer after completing the emergency drills

-          Provide realistic emergency drills that others competitors cannot provide

 

 

Customer Relationships

-          Trust, reliable product, and provide excellent customer service

-          Build relationship on trust and loyalty

-          Build a relationship to have customer purchase apps add-on packs

-          Engaged customer will provide profit through add-on packs

 

Customer Segments

-          General public and corporates

-           All customers are important in a diversified way

 

 

 

Key Resources

-          Developers, proper licensing for Xamarin

-          Microsoft Visual studio c#

-          Google Play and IPhone store fees

 

 

 

 

Channels

-          All Mobile devices

-          Android

-          IOS

-          Web

-          All app stores

-          Google Play cost only $25 to register

-          Key words in The Drill app for search engine to show The Drill app on top ten relevant apps.

 

 

Cost Structure

-          Cost of licenses for Xamarin and cost to hosting the Drill app

-          Amazon / Peer1 hosting / Engine Yard hosting

-          Microsoft Visual Studio licensing

-          IPhone store

Revenue Streams

-          Cost of add-on packs

-          Promotions deals for purchasing The Drill app

-          Free basic app for general public and corporates pay licensing fees

-          Paid through app stores by credit card or pay pal setup
